Function	Protein-histidine N-methyltransferase that specifically catalyzes 1-methylhistidine (pros-methylhistidine) methylation of target proteins (PubMed:33563959, PubMed:34562450, PubMed:37015930, PubMed:37398635). Specifically methylates the second His of proteins with a His-x-His (HxH) motif (where 'x' is preferably a small amino acid), while exploiting the first one as a recognition signature (PubMed:37398635). Catalyzes methylation of target proteins such as S100A9, NDUFB3, SLC39A5, SLC39A7, ARMC6 and DNAJB12; 1-methylhistidine modification may affect the binding of zinc and other metals to its target proteins (PubMed:33563959, PubMed:34562450, PubMed:37015930, PubMed:37398635). Constitutes the main methyltransferase for the 1-methylhistidine modification in cell (PubMed:33563959).
