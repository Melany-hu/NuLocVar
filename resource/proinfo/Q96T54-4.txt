Function	K(+) channel that conducts voltage-dependent outward rectifying currents upon membrane depolarization. Homo- and heterodimerizes to form functional channels with distinct regulatory and gating properties (PubMed:36063992). Present in the cardiac conduction system where it may regulate action potential duration and beating frequency of cardiac myocytes (PubMed:24972929). Permeable to other monovalent cations such as Rb(+) and Cs(+) (By similarity) (PubMed:26919430).
